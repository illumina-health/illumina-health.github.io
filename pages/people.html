A Collaborative Foundation

Illumina Health Lab was founded in 2026 through a cross-institutional collaboration between researchers and students at Harvard Medical School, Rutgers Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy, and Duke University. Each member brings a distinct disciplinary lens — medicine, pharmacy, data science, public health — forming a research team uniquely positioned to address the interconnected challenges of modern healthcare.
